# Stories of Love

Size: 74" x 93" 
Time taken: 4 months 

Made entirely of little Zhara's clothes - a quilt for her grandparents. This quilt is a 
celebration of intergenerational love; the priceless bond of a grandchild with her fun-
loving grandparents; and the unconditional love of parents who made this quilt 
possible. 

So grateful to Zhara’s parents for trusting me with this project, by giving me complete 
freedom to design without any time constraints… but above all, thank you to them for 
sharing those lovely stories: of their parents, Zhara's bond with them, and their love 
for animals - all of which helped drive the design for this quilt. 

I have had so much fun making this precious piece. Endless gratitude. 

The quilt blocks are detailed using the running-stitch - a traditional hand-quilting 
technique, reminiscent of the hand embroidery on Sindhi rallis - that binds the two 
cotton layers into a cohesive whole.
